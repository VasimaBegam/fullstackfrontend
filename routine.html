💡 Quick Tips

  • Add Plan: Creates a shiny new routine entry inside your active tracker account.
  • View All: Fetches your complete routine list saved on the server cloud.
  • Editing Data: Use Update to replace the full item, or Modify to edit just single lines.

📋 Plan Properties

Each routine plan contains these fields:

  • 🔢 Plan ID: A unique number key
  • 🏷️ Title Name: Title or focus area
  • 📊 Difficulty: Target fitness level tier
  • 🌍 Visibility: Shared or private flag
